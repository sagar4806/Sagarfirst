About Me

I'm Sagar from Siddhpur, India, and I've always been determined to pursue a career in the world of technology and data science. My journey began with a modest 90 marks in my 10th-grade CBSE board exams. Undeterred, I decided to delve into computer engineering by completing a diploma a GPG Gandhinagar, where I honed my skills and passion for the field.

With a strong foundation, I set my sights on further education, eventually earning both a bachelor's and master's degree in Computer Science from the prestigious University of Waterloo in Canada. During my time there, I delved into the fascinating realm of Data Science, acquiring the knowledge and expertise needed to excel in this dynamic field.

Today, I proudly serve as a Data Science Engineer at Bifroat.ai, where I have the privilege of applying my skills to real-world challenges, contributing to cutting-edge projects, and making a meaningful impact in the world of data science. My journey has been a testament to my dedication to learning and my passion for technology, and I look forward to continuing to grow and innovate in this ever-evolving field.
